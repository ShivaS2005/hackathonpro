# 🤖 AI Chat - Quick Reference Card

## What Is It?

An intelligent AI Task Assistant that understands natural language and helps you manage tasks efficiently through a beautiful chat interface.

---

## Quick Start (30 seconds)

1. **Login** to your dashboard
2. **Find** "AI Assistant" section
3. **Click** a quick button OR type a question
4. **Get** instant intelligent responses

---

## 6 Main Features

### ✨ New Tasks
**Ask:** "Show new tasks"  
**Get:** Recently assigned pending tasks  
**Use:** Check what you just got assigned

### 📅 Due Today
**Ask:** "What's due today?"  
**Get:** Tasks due on current date  
**Use:** Plan your day

### 🎯 Reschedule
**Ask:** "Reschedule my priorities"  
**Get:** Tasks sorted by urgency & deadline  
**Use:** Get focused & productive

### 🔥 Urgent
**Ask:** "Show urgent tasks"  
**Get:** High-priority critical work  
**Use:** Stay on top of critical items

### ✅ Completed
**Ask:** "Show my completed tasks"  
**Get:** Recently finished work  
**Use:** Review progress

### 📊 Summary
**Ask:** "Give me a summary"  
**Get:** Task statistics & overview  
**Use:** Understand your workload

- Combine both approaches
