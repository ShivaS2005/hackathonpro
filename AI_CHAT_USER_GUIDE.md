# 🤖 AI Task Assistant - Quick Start Guide

## What Is the AI Task Assistant?

Your personal AI assistant that understands natural language questions about your tasks and provides intelligent responses to help you manage your workload efficiently.

## How to Access It

### For Employees
1. Login to Employee Dashboard
2. Click on **"AI Assistant"** tab
3. Start asking questions!

### For Employers
1. Login to Employer Dashboard
2. Go to **"Task Management"** tab
3. Scroll to **"AI Assistant"** section
4. Ask your questions!

## What You Can Ask

### 1. Show New Tasks
**Say:** "Show me new tasks" or "What's new?" or "New assignments"

**AI Will:**
- List all your newly assigned pending tasks
- Show task name, description, due date
- Display category and status
- Sort by creation date (newest first)

**Best For:** Checking what you just got assigned

---

### 2. Tasks Due Today
**Say:** "What's due today?" or "Today's tasks" or "Due today"

**AI Will:**
- Show all tasks due on today's date
- Exclude already completed tasks
- Display full task details
- Help you focus on immediate deadlines

**Best For:** Planning your day in the morning

---

### 3. Reschedule Priorities
**Say:** "Reschedule my priorities" or "Reorganize tasks" or "What should I do first?"

**AI Will:**
- Analyze all your active tasks
- Sort by urgency (urgent tasks first)
- Then sort by deadline (soonest first)
- Show optimized task order
- Help you maximize productivity

**Best For:** Getting organized and focused work session

---

### 4. Show Urgent Tasks
**Say:** "Show urgent tasks" or "What's urgent?" or "Critical work"

**AI Will:**
- Filter only urgent category tasks
- Show non-completed items
- Display all task details
- Help you stay on top of critical work

**Best For:** Finding what needs immediate attention

---

### 5. Completed Tasks
**Say:** "Show my completed tasks" or "What did I finish?" or "Completed work"

**AI Will:**
- Display recently finished tasks
- Show latest 10 completions
- Highlight your achievements
- Celebrate progress

**Best For:** Reviewing accomplishments and staying motivated

---

### 6. Task Summary
**Say:** "Give me a summary" or "Task overview" or "Statistics"

**AI Will:**
- Show total task count
- Count pending tasks
- Count in-progress tasks
- Count completed tasks
- Show urgent task count
- Show overdue task count

**Example Response:**
```
📊 Here's your task summary:
• Total Tasks: 15
• Pending: 8
• In Progress: 4
• Completed: 3
• Urgent: 2
• Overdue: 1
```

**Best For:** Getting a high-level overview of your workload

## Tips & Tricks

### 💡 Use Natural Language
The AI understands many ways to ask the same thing:
- "Show new tasks" = "What's new?" = "New assignments"
- "Due today" = "Today's tasks" = "What's due?"
- "Reschedule" = "Reorganize" = "What should I do first?"

### ⚡ Mix Questions with Buttons
- Click a button to see results
- Then ask a follow-up question
- Mix manual and automated queries

### 🎯 Ask Follow-Ups
After seeing tasks:
- "Reschedule them for me" → Get optimal order
- "Show only urgent ones" → Filter results
- "What's the deadline?" → Get specific info

### 📱 Mobile Friendly
Works great on mobile devices:
- Touch quick buttons easily
- Swipe to scroll messages
- Responsive design adapts to screen

## Understanding Task Display

When AI shows tasks, you'll see:

```
📌 Task Name
[URGENT] [PENDING] Due: 12/31/2024
Full task description text here...
```

**Color Codes:**
- **Red** = URGENT (do this first!)
- **Orange** = MEDIUM (important)
- **Green** = LEAST (lower priority)

**Status Indicators:**
- **Blue** = PENDING (waiting to start)
- **Orange** = IN-PROGRESS (currently working)
- **Green** = COMPLETED (finished)

## Common Questions

**Q: Can I ask anything?**
A: Best for task-related questions. For other topics, AI suggests task-focused queries.

**Q: Does it understand typos?**
A: Yes! The AI is smart about common variations and typos.

**Q: Can I type weird questions?**
A: Try it! AI will try to understand. If confused, it asks for clarification.

**Q: Is my data private?**
A: Yes! AI only shows YOU your tasks. Employers see only their assigned tasks.

**Q: How fast is it?**
A: Usually responds in less than 1 second!

**Q: Can I use voice?**
A: Not yet, but type your questions for now.

**Q: Does it create/delete tasks?**
A: Currently it only shows and reorganizes. More features coming!

## Troubleshooting

**Problem: AI says "couldn't process your request"**
- Solution: Check your internet connection
- Try again with a simpler question

**Problem: Tasks not showing up**
- Solution: Make sure tasks are created first
- Check if assigned to you

**Problem: Different results than expected**
- Solution: Try more specific wording
- Use buttons instead of typing

**Problem: Takes too long to respond**
- Solution: Refresh the page
- Check internet connection
- Try a simpler query
